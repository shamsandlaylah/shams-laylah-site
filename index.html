Building faith-grounded childhood through joyful storytelling

Shams & Laylah is a faith-centered children’s storytelling platform helping the next generation grow in iman, character, and confidence — at home, in schools, and in masajid.

We create original puppet characters, songs, live experiences, and classroom-ready resources — and partner with mission-aligned organizations to expand joyful Islamic learning into everyday childhood.
